At Task Manager, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name and email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Profile picture (optional)

Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your device and usage:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • IP address
  • Pages visited and features used
  • Time spent on the application

Task Data

We store the tasks and projects you create, including:

  • Task titles and descriptions
  • Due dates and priorities
  • Categories and tags
  • Completion status

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Personalize your experience
  • Send important notifications about your tasks
  • Improve our AI recommendations
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  • Respond to customer service requests
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit
  • Encryption at rest for sensitive data
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Secure data centers with 24/7 monitoring

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services

We may share your information with third-party services that help us operate our application:

  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns
  • Email Services: To send notifications
  • Cloud Storage: To securely store your data
  • Payment Processors: For premium subscriptions

These services are bound by their own privacy policies and we ensure they meet our security standards.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a readable format
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
